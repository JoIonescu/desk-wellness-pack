# 🧘 Desk Wellness Pack

A lightweight browser-based toolkit designed to improve focus, reduce fatigue, and promote healthier work habits throughout the day.

Built for people who spend long hours at their desk and want subtle, non-intrusive support for better wellbeing.

---

## ✨ Overview

Desk Wellness Pack is a collection of micro-interventions that help you stay physically and mentally balanced while working.

Instead of overwhelming users with complex tracking or dashboards, this project focuses on **small, timely nudges** that fit naturally into your workflow.

---

## 🎯 Goals

- Reduce eye strain and physical fatigue
- Encourage regular breaks and movement
- Support mental clarity and focus
- Promote healthier desk habits without distraction

---

## 🧩 Features

- ⏱️ Smart break reminders (e.g. 20-20-20 rule)
- 🧍 Movement & posture nudges
- 💧 Hydration prompts
- 🌿 Micro wellness suggestions (breathing, reset moments)
- 🔕 Minimal, non-intrusive notifications
- ⚙️ Configurable frequency and intensity
